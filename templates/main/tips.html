General Eco-Friendly Travel Tips

Use Public Transport

Mumbai’s trains, buses, and metros are efficient and reduce individual emissions. Opt for them over personal vehicles whenever possible.

Switch to Electric Vehicles (EVs)

Electric cars, bikes, and scooters have lower emissions than gasoline or diesel vehicles. With growing EV infrastructure, making the switch helps reduce your carbon footprint.

Walk or Cycle for Short Distances

Walking or cycling for trips under 3 km can eliminate emissions entirely while improving your health. Mumbai’s developing cycling lanes make this easier.

Use Fuel-efficient Vehicles

Keep your vehicle well-maintained to optimize fuel efficiency. If possible, switch to compressed natural gas (CNG), which emits less carbon than petrol or diesel.

Carpool and Ride-share

Use carpooling apps or share rides with friends and colleagues. This cuts down on the number of vehicles on the road and reduces emissions per passenger.

Eco-friendly Navigation

Use navigation apps to find less congested, fuel-efficient routes. Also, avoid unnecessary idling, as it wastes fuel and increases emissions.
